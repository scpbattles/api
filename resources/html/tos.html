VIII. Code of conduct

As mentioned in section V. Account Terminations and Suspections, SCP Battles may terminate or suspend your account for violations of this code of conduct, and the sole discretion of SCP Battles. The following rules may result in either a termination or suspension depending on the severity of the violation, as determined by the SCP Battles administrators and/or moderators.

  1. Obscene Content may result in a ban or suspension. This includes attempts to bypass any language filters that may or may not be implemented in the future.
  2. Scamming In or outside of SCP Battles, with the intent to steal user information may result in a ban or suspension. Scamming includes ANY form of deception with the intent to steal account credentials.
  3. Spamming may result in a ban or suspension.
  4. Hacking will result in a permanent account termination. Hacking includes any alterations to the client with the intent to provide an unfair advantage to the player or to “unlock” content that is intended to be restricted to the user.
  5. Violations of user privacy may result in a ban or account suspension. This includes posting any information about a user without their consent such as their name, IP address, physical location, or any other “family” or “real-life” information that may intimidate or disturb the user. “Doxxing” is prohibited and may result in account termination.
